Jacob Seiden - Chief Operating Officer

Hello there! My name is Jacob Seiden and I am the Chief Operating Officer here at Retro Stars LLC

I have decades of experience working on the managerial and operations side of brick and mortar video game retailers and I simply wasn't passionate about what I was doing. I felt like I could be doing more to help the community that has been the backbone of the industry that I have devoted my career to. Which is the exact reason why I helped start Retro Stars LLC

My duties as Chief Operating Officer of Retro Stars LLC include quality assurance and making sure that we are always putting our beloved customers first. I am also tasked with maintaining our team of appraisers in order to ensure that our customers are getting the fairset price for the collectibles that they hold near and dear to their heart
